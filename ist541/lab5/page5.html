Finding a Route

Part of the joy of running is enjoying the environment you're running in. If you find the sights, sounds, and smells of your typical route stressful or boring, then it's time to find a new trail.

Open space parks are excellent running locations. They give your joints a break from the hard pavement, give your ears a rest from the city's hustle and bustle, gift your lungs with fresher air, stimulate your mind with winding trails, and challenge your body with some ups and downs. The Bay Area offers regional open space parks in all corners of the bay. Check out some of the offerings below.
